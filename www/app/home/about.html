mPowerYOUth App

This mobile app "mPowerYOUth" will bring Mobile-based YOuth Empowering and Counselling services.

It was developed as a part of Mobile App Development contest organised by G2C Office during June-July 2015. The App was selected under the contest category of "Youth and Women" with the following objectives:
1. Enhance access to public service delivery for core services (Youth & Women) through mobile platform; and
2. Promote innovation and creativity for domestic mobile apps development in service delivery.


The App Development Contest is kindly sponsored by Swiss Cooperation Office in Bhutan under the Democratic Governance Plus (DG+) Programme.



Department of Youths and Sports and Youth Development Fund shall remain the sole content provider for this App.
